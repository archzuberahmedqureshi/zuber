Ar. Zuber Ahmed & Associates

Ar. Zuber Ahmed, founder of Ar. Zuber Ahmed & Associates, brings over 20 years of expertise in creating thoughtful, functional, and timeless architectural solutions. A graduate of the School of Architecture, Indore, he began his career under the mentorship of renowned architect Hafeez Contractor in Mumbai, gaining invaluable exposure to large-scale and diverse projects.

With projects across Indore, Bhopal, Dewas, Mumbai, Ajmer, Kota, Nagpur,Shirdi, Agra and beyond, the firm has built a legacy of designing spaces that seamlessly merge aesthetics, practicality, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To shape meaningful spaces that go beyond structures — crafting designs that inspire, nurture communities, and leave a lasting legacy.

Our Vision

To be a leading architectural practice recognized for innovation, integrity, and sustainable design that transforms cities and enriches lives.
